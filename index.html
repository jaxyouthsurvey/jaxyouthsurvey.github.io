Kids Hope Alliance has teamed up with NLP Logix and the Nonprofit Center of NE Florida to document the needs of children, youth, and their families here in Jacksonville. A key piece of this project is listening to you: the youth, parents, caregivers, and service providers. You live the everyday experience of raising and caring for the next generation in Jacksonville.
Thank you for your time and for adding your voice to this important project!
Your responses to this survey will help guide our leaders to meet the needs of our community.
